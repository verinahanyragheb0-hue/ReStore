Warranty Policy

Your satisfaction and trust are our top priorities. Here is how our warranty works.

1. Warranty Coverage

All refurbished and repaired items purchased through ReStore come with a standard 12-month limited warranty covering hardware and technical defects.

2. What is Not Covered?

The warranty does not cover accidental drops, liquid damage, unauthorized repairs, or general wear and tear from normal daily usage.

3. How to Claim Warranty

Simply navigate to your profile dashboard, find your order ID, and click on "File a Warranty Claim". Our team will review it within 48 hours.
