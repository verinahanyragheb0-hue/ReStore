Responsible E-Waste Disposal

Don't throw away old tech. Recycle your broken or obsolete electronics safely with ReStore to protect our environment from toxic waste.

Recycling Guidelines

Before you hand over your devices, please make sure to follow these safety steps:

  • Data Reset: Factory reset your phones and laptops to remove personal data.
  • Remove Batteries: If possible, remove loose or swollen batteries safely.
  • No Accessories: Keep your personal cases and memory cards at home.

Why Recycle with Us?

  • Prevents hazardous chemicals from leaking into land and water.
  • Helps recover valuable copper, silver, and gold components.
  • Reduces carbon emissions caused by manufacturing new parts.
